KEYNOTE SPEAKERS

Mr. Javed Akhtar

Javed Akhtar stands as an iconic figure in the realm of Indian arts and culture. With an acclaimed lyrical prowess and a pen that weaves stories of human emotions, his creativity transcends boundaries.

Known for his work in Hindi cinema, he has won five National Film Awards, and received the Padma Shri in 1999 and the Padma Bhushan in 2007, two of India's highest civilian honors.

Mr. Rajat Sharma

Rajat Sharma is recognized as one of the most influential media personalities in India. With an unwavering pursuit of truth and a heart of valor, he has carved a niche for himself and won the hearts of millions through the highly acclaimed show, “Aap Ki Adalat”.

His legacy, spanning years of dedication, has garnered him numerous accolades, including the esteemed Padma Bhushan.
